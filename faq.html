Everything you need to know before booking your journey. Can't find what you're looking for? We're just a message away.

Booking & Payments

How do I book a travel package?
Simply browse our Destinations or Packages page, select your preferred trip, and click "Book Now." Follow the step-by-step instructions in our secure checkout to enter traveler details and complete your reservation.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept all major Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), UPI, and direct Net Banking. All transactions are encrypted and processed through secure payment gateways.

Cancellations & Refunds

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?
Yes. Cancellations made 15 days prior to departure are eligible for a full refund (minus a small service fee). Rescheduling depends on hotel and flight availability; please contact our support team for specific requests.

Travel Documents

What documents are required for international travel?
You will typically need a passport valid for at least 6 months, a valid visa for your destination, and travel insurance. We provide a detailed checklist of required documents for every international booking.

Still have questions?

Our dedicated travel experts are available 24/7 to assist you with any specific queries you may have.
